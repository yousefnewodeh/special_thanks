To the ones who stayed.

You didn't have to. But you did.
Every single time.

expand_more

When I had nothing left,
you showed up.

There were nights when the air felt heavy and the room felt far too large. I expected to find myself alone in that silence, as most would have found reason to leave. Instead, you were there, sitting quietly in the corner of my mess, refusing to let the shadows be the only company I kept.

You didn't try to fix the unfixable. You simply stayed, anchoring me when I was drifting toward shores I didn't recognize. That quiet presence meant more than a thousand grand gestures ever could.

You gave me things I didn't know I needed.

visibility

You believed in me

Even when my own reflection felt like a stranger, you saw the parts of me worth keeping. You held onto my potential until I was strong enough to reach for it myself.

eco

Space to fall apart

You never asked me to be "on." You let me be broken, messy, and silent, without once making me feel like I was taking up too much room in your life.

anchor

You never gave up

Through the repetitive cycles and the redundant apologies, you remained constant. Your loyalty wasn't a transaction; it was a sanctuary I could always return to.

I still think about these.

The time you stayed when I needed someone most

Every time you checked on me without being asked

The way you never made me feel like a burden

Dearest Friend,

I am writing this because often, in the middle of our days, I forget to tell you how much of my world is held together by your presence. You have held space for me when I couldn't even stand within it myself. You've witnessed the unpolished, uncurated versions of my life and you never once turned your eyes away.

Thank you for the silence we shared, for the words you chose so carefully, and for the times you simply sat there, reminding me that I was not alone in the dark. You are the quiet miracle I never expected, but always needed.

With all my love,

Yousef

Thank you for not letting go.

You are more loved than you know.

For the ones who stayed at 2am. You know who you are.
